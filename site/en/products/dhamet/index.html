Dhamet

A digital application for the Mauritanian game Dhamet, built for clear play, learning, and practice through the browser.

The app preserves the original spirit and known rules of the game while making it easier to learn, play, and follow.

Open app

What the app offers

The app provides a digital playing experience for the traditional game, allowing users to play matches under clear rules with tools that help them follow the board and understand the game state. It supports a simplified interface, suitable play modes, and explanations of core rules such as the opening, movement, capture, Souvla, and promotion to Dhaaem.

The experience stays focused on the game itself, without unnecessary complexity or elements that do not support learning and playing.

The terms keep the official rules as the operational reference for the digital version.

App features

  • Play Dhamet in the browser without installation.
  • Play against the computer for training and practice.
  • Choose the computer level.
  • Play online with other players.
  • Send and accept game invitations.
  • Create a match room when an invitation is accepted.
  • Synchronize the match state between both players.
  • Watch some ongoing matches when available.
  • Create an account and sign in.
  • Google sign-in when enabled.
  • Player profile with nickname and avatar.
  • Save account-linked results.
  • Score and statistics tracking.
  • Player ranking based on results and statistics.
  • Move history for following the game sequence.
  • Local save and resume on the same browser/device.
  • Language, appearance, and preference settings.
  • Arabic, English, and French support.
  • Switch languages during use.
  • Mandatory opening logic.
  • Illegal move prevention.
  • Movement rules based on point and path type.
  • Captures and chained captures.
  • Button to end a capture sequence.
  • Souvla rule support.
  • Promotion to Dhaaem at the last row.
  • Win and draw handling.
  • Match tools such as undo, new game, and exit.
  • Online refresh button for resynchronization.
  • Text chat inside online matches.
  • Voice chat when enabled.
  • Microphone and sound controls.
  • Leave online rooms.
  • Local data storage for a better experience.
  • Clear local browser data when desired.
  • Account deletion option.
  • A digital version that preserves the original rules.
