Prayer times and alerts

Al-Moathen El-Chinguitti

A standalone Android app for prayer times, adhan, Qibla, and devotional reminders with a Mauritanian Chinguitti identity.

It shows the next prayer and countdown, provides precise calculation and alert controls, and retains a local calculation fallback when the network is unavailable.

Download and install

What the app provides

The home screen combines a clear clock, the next prayer, a countdown, Gregorian and Hijri dates, the selected city, and the five daily prayer times. The user can select a country and city manually or optionally use location to find the nearest listed city.

The app also includes a Qibla compass and an extensive alert system for prayers, daily and weekly devotional activities, and Islamic seasons. It supports custom reminders, bundled audio, or a sound file selected from the phone.

Privacy policy

Explains local settings storage, optional location, the coordinates and calculation choices sent to the AlAdhan service during online updates, and the permissions used for notifications, exact alarms, and adhan playback.

Verified features

  • Next prayer with a countdown updated every second.
  • Fajr, Dhuhr, Asr, Maghrib, and Isha times.
  • Gregorian and Hijri dates with a dual-calendar date picker.
  • Manual city selection or optional nearest-city detection.
  • MWL, Egypt, Makkah, Umm Al-Qura, and ISNA methods.
  • Shafi‘i or Hanafi Asr and high-latitude options.
  • Per-prayer minute corrections and Hijri-date correction.
  • Monthly online updates with cached and local calculation fallbacks.
  • Qibla compass using location and phone orientation sensors.
  • Prayer, voluntary worship, adhkar, Friday, fasting, and seasonal alerts.
  • Ramadan, Dhul Hijjah, Ashura, and white-days reminders.
  • One to five randomized daily reminders.
  • One-time, daily, weekly, and Hijri custom reminders.
  • Sound, vibration, or silent mode for each alert.
  • Bundled Mauritanian adhan and recitation audio, plus local audio import.
  • Arabic, French, and English with light and dark modes.
  • Font, text size, numeral style, and default-calendar controls.
  • No account and no advertising.

Safer installation

  1. Download the file only from this official page.
  2. Open it and temporarily allow installation from the app you used when Android asks.
  3. Allow Google Play Protect to scan the file, and do not install it if the system identifies it as harmful.
  4. Turn off “Allow from this source” again after installation.
  5. Review location, notification, and exact-alarm permissions in system settings and enable only what you need.
