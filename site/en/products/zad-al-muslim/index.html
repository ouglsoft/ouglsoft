Daily worship companion

Zad Al-Muslim

An Android app that brings Qur’an, adhkar, supplications, prayer times, Qibla, daily goals, and practical worship tools into one organized experience.

It keeps core content and personal progress on the device, works in Arabic, English, and French, and requires no account or advertising.

Download and installation

What version 1.1.0 provides

The home screen organizes today’s deeds, Qur’an, adhkar, supplications, prayer and Qibla tools, the dual calendar and night times, plus dedicated Ramadan, Friday, Eid, and Islamic knowledge sections.

You can create a Qur’an wird and track a khatma, save favourites, use the smart tasbih, estimate zakat, track Ramadan makeup fasts, and create a personal worship plan. This progress remains local and is not cloud-synced.

Privacy policy

Explains local storage, optional location, the data sent to AlAdhan during online timetable updates, on-device sensors, and the absence of accounts, advertising, and in-app analytics.

Verified features

  • Organized home screen for daily deeds, content, tools, and seasons.
  • Offline Qur’an reader with search and page navigation.
  • Daily Qur’an wird, khatma goals, and progress tracking.
  • Documented adhkar and supplications with search and favourites.
  • Smart tasbih with a target and optional vibration.
  • Zakat tools for money, trade, gold, silver, crops, and livestock.
  • Prayer times, Qibla, and Gregorian and Hijri calendars.
  • Night portions, milestones, duration, and moon phase.
  • Ramadan, Friday, and Eid sections with makeup-fast tracking.
  • Names of Allah, ruqyah, virtues, etiquette, and concise rulings.
  • Personal worship plan and daily completion checklist.
  • Global search, favourites, and customizable pinned shortcuts.
  • Arabic, English, and French.
  • Light and dark themes with font, size, and numeral controls.
  • Manual city selection or optional location.
  • No account, advertising, or in-app analytics SDK.

Calculator results are indicative estimates, not a fatwa or accounting advice. Details vary by circumstances and qualified guidance.

Progress, favourites, and plans stay on the phone. Clearing app data or uninstalling may remove them permanently.

Safer installation

  1. Download the file only from this official page.
  2. Open it and temporarily allow installation from the app you used when Android asks.
  3. Allow Google Play Protect to scan the file, and do not install it if the system identifies it as harmful.
  4. Turn off “Allow from this source” again after installation.
  5. Review location and vibration permissions in system settings and enable only what you need.
