Mauritanian strategy game

Dhamet

A distinctive digital version of the traditional Mauritanian strategy game Dhamet. It is not a standard checkers clone; it brings a rare point-and-path board game to the web with mandatory opening logic, captures, promotion, and the distinctive Souvla rule.

It gives new players a clear way to learn the rules and gives experienced players a digital space to practice, play against the computer, or challenge others online while preserving the game’s Mauritanian character.

  • Traditional 9×9 point board logic
  • Play against the computer
  • Online play and spectating when available
  • Guest access, email sign-in, and Google sign-in

Qur’an, adhkar and worship tools

Zad Al-Muslim

A daily worship companion for Qur’an, adhkar, supplications, prayer times, Qibla, daily goals, tasbih, zakat, and Islamic seasons.

Includes khatma progress, favourites, personal plans, offline core content, three languages, and light and dark themes.

  • Qur’an reader, daily wird, and khatma tracking
  • Adhkar, supplications, search, and favourites
  • Tasbih, zakat, Ramadan, and personal plans
  • No account and no advertising

Prayer times and alerts

Al-Moathen El-Chinguitti

A standalone Android app for prayer times, adhan, and Qibla, with daily, weekly, seasonal, and custom reminders.

Supports manual city or optional location, several calculation methods, time corrections, local fallback, Mauritanian audio, and three interface languages.

  • Next prayer and live countdown
  • Sensor-based Qibla with calibration guidance
  • Flexible sound, vibration, or silent alerts
  • No account and no advertising

Monthly spending guard and budget target

Safe-to-Spend

A monthly spending guard for tracking safe-to-spend, daily pace, budget targets, exclusions, transactions, and accounts in a clear visual structure.

It helps the user see how much remains safe to spend this month, whether spending is moving too fast, and how budgets compare with last month and the year average.

  • Safe-to-spend monthly guard and daily pace
  • Budget targets, month forecast, and spend pressure
  • Exclusions for transactions that should not affect the guard
  • Backup, CSV/TSV/TXT import/export, and history
