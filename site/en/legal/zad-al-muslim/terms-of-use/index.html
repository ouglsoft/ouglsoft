4. Prayer times, Qibla and calendars

Prayer times depend on coordinates, city, calculation method, Asr school, high-latitude option, corrections, and time zone. Qibla depends on location and device sensors. Hijri dates, night portions, seasonal countdowns, and moon phases are calculated estimates. Compare results with trusted local authorities when appropriate.
