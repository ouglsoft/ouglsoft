Software development services in Mauritania

OuglSoft develops software for different domains. The company is not limited to one subject area; it designs, builds, reviews, and improves digital products according to the needs of each project.

01

Mobile app development

Design and build Android and mobile-first experiences with clear navigation, stable data flows, release-ready assets, and interfaces suited to the target language and audience.

02

Website development

Create modern company websites, product pages, landing pages, documentation pages, and legal/support pages with responsive layouts and clean content structure.

03

Web platforms and digital interfaces

Develop browser-based tools, dashboards, account systems, public web apps, and structured interfaces that work across devices.

04

Custom software solutions

Build software around a specific workflow instead of forcing the client into a generic template. This can include internal tools, data entry flows, calculators, and operational utilities.

05

UX and product improvement

Review existing apps or websites, identify confusing flows, weak copy, visual inconsistency, and technical friction, then propose or implement improvements.

06

Technical tools by need

Create focused tools for repeated tasks, content management, reporting, import/export, verification, or other practical needs.

07

Digital product audit and analysis

Inspect a product from the user, design, content, and technical angles, then provide a prioritized improvement path based on risk and expected value.
